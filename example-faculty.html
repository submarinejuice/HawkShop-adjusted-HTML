Course Materials Status

The following status indicators are used in the bookstore system to track course materials:

Status Description
Approved Materials have been reviewed and approved for course use
Pending Review Materials submitted and awaiting approval
Not Available Materials not yet received or not available

How to Submit Course Materials:

  1. Log into the Faculty Portal using your Laurier credentials
  2. Navigate to "Course Materials" section
  3. Select your course and fill out the required information
  4. Submit for review and approval
