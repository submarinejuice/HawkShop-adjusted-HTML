Printing Methods

Explore our range of high-quality printing techniques to find the perfect match for your custom project.

Embroidery

A traditional decoration method providing classic, high-quality feel with exceptional detail and texture. Perfect for left/right chest and sleeve applications.

View Examples

Puff Print

Also known as 3D embroidery, creates a dimensional effect using foam substrate. Popular for caps, sweatshirts, jackets and vests for extra visual impact.

View Examples

Emboss

Creates a raised effect that pops out from the surface. Imparts a luxurious tactile quality to your items, making them feel special and premium.

View Examples

Heat Transfer

Perfect for detailed graphics and full-color images. This versatile method applies designs to various fabrics with vibrant results and excellent durability.

View Examples

Screen Printing

Classic technique for durable, vibrant results. Ideal for bulk orders, t-shirts, and promotional items with consistent, high-quality reproduction.

View Examples
